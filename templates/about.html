About Us

There are many plant related diseases which are increasing due to environmental changes, insects and many more. Even farmers may not be have more knowledge or may not be aware of these type of disease and are facing lot of problems in identifying and finding solution for those. This web application is designed to provide immediate suggestions for these problems faced by the farmers. This application provides services to farmers by giving possible remedies and guidance by the expertise.
